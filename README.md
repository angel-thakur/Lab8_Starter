## Service Workers and Graceful Degradation: 
Service workers are like the safety net for an application and its potential failures. Instead of one error nuking the entirety of the application, service workers help the application work in at least a reduced capacity, which is the concept of graceful degradation. The functionality may decrease, but service workers help ensure the product is at least minimally functional from the user's perspective
